# Requirements Call Notes — Staff Procurement Portal

> **How to use:** Fill this in after every requirements or discovery call.
> Save a copy as `CALL_NOTES_YYYY-MM-DD.md` in this folder.
> Then run the BA agent and say:
> "Read `docs/discovery/CALL_NOTES_YYYY-MM-DD.md` and the previous notes in
> `docs/discovery/`. Extract requirements, then interview me one question at a time
> to fill in what's missing. After the interview, produce or update
> `docs/requirements/REQUIREMENTS.md` and `docs/requirements/FUNCTIONAL_DESIGN.md`."
